This story begins with me wanting to write for my own (and others) a long time ago.
One may ask
> "Why? Writing comes natural, if you sought-after?"
but for me, it never worked.

To this question I can only give you the following answer.
I always got caught in some kind of perfectionism, i.e., I never found the right tool for me, which prevented me from starting all along.
Once I tried [hugo](https://gohugo.io/), but I was not happy with it.
Then I tried to build my own static-site-generator but stopped half-way through and deleted, what I had.
Most of the time I wanted to share all the nerdy tech tools I tinkered with, but without a platform to share, it never had been.

But with new plans running through my family, I wanted to just get this out of my head and document it, and so I'm back at [hugo](https://gohugo.io/) just to get it going.
Maybe it is even the fact, that I grew older and embrace the working, but not so perfect tools, rather than the maybe perfect, but not available ones.

Now, some may ask

> "Will this be yet another blog about tech or travel or something else?"

Maybe it will, maybe it won't.
But the thing is, that I noticed that I need some kind of volume to get my head empty and share my thoughts.
Be them about what is currently happening in my life or some nice tooling I discovered.
Moreover, is it meant to *document* our current situation as a family and the things, that are changing for us.
So, I will write it down and try to keep it up to date as time lets me do it.

So the blog will probably span over
- General writing
- Tech
    - Nix and NixOS
    - Linux in general
    - Programming
- Photography

But it may also change over time.
If you will ever have some questions about the things I write about, just [contact me](mailto:contact@audacis.net).
