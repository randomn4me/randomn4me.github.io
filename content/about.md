Hi, I'm Philipp, father of little humans, research assistant at [PEASEC](https://peasec.de) working on making the world more secure.
My current research focus is how to get mitigate information overload in CERTs and SOCs and prepare the used information.
Hereby I use methods of natural language processing, information retrieval, and data mining, tailored to common tasks in information security.
To give you an example, my colleague Markus Bayer and me trained a large language model for the ITSec domain called [CySecBERT](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3652594)[^1].

Besides that, I'm interested in crawling and programming, NixOS (and [using it for all my machines](https://github.com/randomn4me/nix-flakes)), and love riding bikes whenever I find the time to do so. 🚴

If you want to contact me, feel free to leave me a [message](mailto:blog@audacis.net).

[^1]: The review process of our paper was too long to claim CyBERT as title.
