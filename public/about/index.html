About Me - Philipp Kühn

Hi, I’m Philipp, father of four lovely children, research assistant at PEASEC working on making the world more secure. My current research focus is how to get mitigate information overload in CERTs and SOCs and prepare the used information. Hereby I use methods of natural language processing, information retrieval, and data mining, tailored to common tasks in information security. To give you an example, my colleague Markus Bayer and me trained a large language model for the ITSec domain called CySecBERT1.

Besides that, I’m interested in crawling and programming, NixOS (and using it for all my machines), and love riding bikes whenever I find the time to do so. 🚴

If you want to contact me, feel free to leave me a message.
