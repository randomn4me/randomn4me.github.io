Why?

October 7, 2024 2-minute read
Writing

This story begins with me wanting to write for my own (and others) a long time ago. One may ask

“Why? Writing comes natural, if you sought-after?” but for me, it never worked.

To this question I can only give you the following answer. I always got caught in some kind of perfectionism, i.e., I never found the right tool for me and then never getting the start. Once I tried hugo, but it never was meant to be for me. Then I tried to build my own static-site-generator but stopped half-way through and deleted, what I had. I never knew, what should be the topic, that I would like to spread to the world. I didn’t want to give to much details of what I am up to, but wanted to share something. Most of the time I wanted to share all the nerdy tech tools I tinkered with, but without a platform to share, it never had been.

But with new plans flowing through my family, I wanted to just get this out of my head and document it. Now, some may ask

“Will this be yet another blog about tech or travel or something else?”

Maybe it will. But the thing is, that I noticed that I need some kind of volume to get my head empty and share my thoughts. Be them about what is currently happening in my life or some nice tooling I discovered. Moreover, is it meant to document our current situation as a family and the things, that are changing for us. So, I will write it down and try to keep it up to date as time lets me do it.

So the blog will probably span over

  • General writing
  • Tech
    • Nix and NixOS
    • Linux in general
    • Programming
  • Photography

But it may also change over time. If you will ever have some questions about the things I write about, just contact me.
